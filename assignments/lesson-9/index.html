Winter Storm to Freeze Hearts on Valentine's Day!

Jack Frost is out to freeze the hearts of any couples brave enough to venture out on Valentine's Day this year. The high is only expected to be 18°F, and the wind will be 45mph from the East, giving us a windchill factor of -4.7°F which is -20.4°C. So our recommendation for couples is to cuddle up safe and sound inside instead of braving those frigid temperatures.
